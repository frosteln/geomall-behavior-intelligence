Hidden cost checklist

  • ALB: AWS public pricing examples show $0.0225/hour plus $0.008 per LCU-hour. Fixed fee only is about $3.90/month at your 8h/day schedule, or $16.43/month if left on.
  • EBS gp3: AWS public pricing examples show $0.08 per GB-month plus optional charges above baseline for extra IOPS and throughput. A 100 GB root disk is roughly $8/month at that reference rate.
  • S3: AWS documents that same-Region EC2 ↔ S3 transfer is free, and the first 100 GB/month of internet data transfer out is free across AWS services.
  • S3 Singapore reference: AWS public announcements list S3 Standard-IA in Asia Pacific (Singapore) at $0.0138 per GB-month.
  • Approval note: verify the exact live Singapore S3 Standard storage and request-tier pricing in the AWS Pricing Calculator before sign-off.
  • Internet egress and cross-AZ data transfer
  • Public IPv4 charges
  • CloudWatch logs and retention
  • DNS hosted zone and query costs

Scenario fit

Normal instance

Use one g4dn.12xlarge when you need the full stack and stable uptime. Business-hours cost is $43.79/day and $218.96/week.

Spot instance

Best when cost matters more than stability and you can relaunch or recover after interruption. Treat the price as a moving band, not a constant.

1 GPU scenario

Only practical for Whisper-only, BERT-only, or a narrow prototype. It is not enough for the complete revised topology, but it is inexpensive at $5.89/day.

8 GPU scenario

Use two 4-GPU nodes when you need active/active throughput, blue/green upgrades, or workload separation. Business-hours cost is $87.58/day and $437.92/week.
